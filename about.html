About Me

The Person Behind
the Code

I'm Jas Strokus — a software engineer at Microsoft, based out of Northern Virginia. I graduated from George Mason University's Volgenau School of Engineering and Computing in 2023.

I've spent my career building software at the intersection of cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and distributed systems. My focus has been on implant and exploit development, offensive cyber operations, vulnerability research, and zero trust policy. Beyond security, I have deep experience with data structures and algorithms, real-time distributed system architecture, 5G mesh networking, cryptography, and low-level development.

"The beautiful thing about building stuff is that you put a piece of yourself, your story, your individualistic perspective into something for the world."

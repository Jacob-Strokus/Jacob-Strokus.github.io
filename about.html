About Me

The Person Behind
the Code

Senior Site Reliability Engineer at Microsoft with a decade of experience spanning offensive cyber operations, AI/ML development, and advanced software engineering. My technical journey began in 2014, evolving from foundational programming to architecting enterprise-scale reliability solutions and developing sophisticated AI-driven infrastructure.

Previously recognized at Parsons Corporation with both the Game Changer (2023) and Standing Ovation (2024) awards for pioneering AI/ML innovations and cyber program expansion. My work has focused on pushing the boundaries of what's possible in infrastructure automation and system reliability.

With a BS in Computer Science from George Mason University and concentrations in Cybersecurity, Mathematics, and Physics, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to solving complex technical challenges. My competitive spirit has led to notable achievements in international cybersecurity competitions, including placing in the top 4% globally at Cyber Apocalypse 2021 (437th out of 12,000+ teams).
